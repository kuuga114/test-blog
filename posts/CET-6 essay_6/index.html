二、 核心名词分类整理

🏆 预测热词 (2026.6 考季重点)

  • 健康与生活: obesity (肥胖), disease (疾病), unbalanced (不均衡的), diet (饮食), physical activity (体力活动), genetic factor (遗传因素), interplay (相互作用), chronic (慢性的), metabolic (代谢性的), public health (公共卫生)
  • 生态与环境: unprecedented (前所未有的), wetland (湿地), diverse (多样的), plain (平原), highland/plateau (高原), valuable (珍贵的), restoration (修复), show/radiate (焕发), ecological (生态的), vitality (活力), sustainable development (可持续发展)
  • 建筑与文化: Temple of Heaven (天坛), Temple of Earth (地坛), nail (铁钉), pray (祈求), sacred/holy (神圣的), architecture (建筑), architect (建筑师)
  • 社会现象: rat race (内卷式竞争), virtuous cycle (良性循环)

📊 词频居中 & 📉 词频较低 (精选)

  • 抽象概念: virtue (美德), atmosphere (氛围), principle (准则), right (权益), idea (理念), consumption (消费), benefit (效益), character (品格)
  • 科技与工程: scientific research (科研), network (网络), project (工程), astronaut (宇航员), orbit (轨道), artificial intelligence (人工智能), navigation system (导航系统)
  • 自然与社会: water resource (水资源), population (人口), ecological environment (生态环境), port (港口), custom (习俗), bamboo (竹子), urban agglomeration (城市群)
  • 文化与艺术 (低频): statue (雕像), stone lion (石狮), corridor (长廊), relic (遗迹), calligraphy (书法), ornament (装饰物), manuscript (手稿)
  • 地理 (低频): canyon (峡谷), glacier (冰川), grassland (草地), species (物种), mineral (矿藏), hot spring (温泉)

三、 四六级翻译通用热词分类 (按主题)

01. 中国文化概述

  • 核心: cultural heritage (文化遗产), outstanding folk arts (优秀民间艺术), patriotism (爱国主义), cultural relics (文物)
  • 人物: the Chinese ancestors (华夏祖先), men of letters (文人), refined scholar (雅士)
  • 常用短语: cradle of civilization (文明摇篮), extensive and profound (博大精深), be honored as (被誉为), play a significant role (占据重要地位), cultural exchange (文化交流), cultural deposit (文化底蕴), traditional virtues (传统美德), symbolic meaning (象征意义)

02. 中国传统文化

  • 工艺: traditional handicraft (传统工艺品), vivid figure (造型生动), unique style (风格独特)
  • 中医/发明: traditional Chinese medicine (中医药), the four great inventions (四大发明), gunpowder (火药), printing (印刷术), compass (指南针)
  • 文房/艺术: Four Treasures of the Study (文房四宝), calligraphy (书法), Chinese brush painting (水墨画), Peking Opera (京剧), shadow play (皮影戏), acrobatics (杂技)
  • 思想/建筑: Confucianism (儒家思想), temple (寺庙), memorial archway (牌坊), Terracotta Warriors (兵马俑), the Silk Road (丝绸之路)

03. 中国传统节日

  • 节日: Spring Festival (春节), Lantern Festival (元宵节), Tomb-sweeping Day (清明节), Dragon Boat Festival (端午节), Mid-autumn Festival (中秋节)
  • 习俗: reunion dinner (年夜饭), pay a new year call (拜年), sweep away ill fortune (驱厄运), red envelopes (红包), spring couplets (春联), firecracker (爆竹), dragon dance (舞龙), lantern riddle (灯谜), enjoy the full moon (赏月), dragon boat race (赛龙舟)

04. 食物及烹饪方法

  • 烹饪动词: fry (煎), stir-fry (炒), deep-fry (炸), stew (炖), steam (蒸), boil (煮), roast (烤)
  • 刀工: cut to strips (切条), dice (切丁), mince (切碎)
  • 具体食物: rice cake (年糕), soy milk (豆浆), steamed bun (馒头), noodles (拉面), tofu (豆腐), hot pot (火锅), Beijing roast duck (北京烤鸭), Chinese Baijiu (白酒)

05. 中国历史

  • 通用: one of the four ancient civilizations (四大文明古国之一), have a long history (历史悠久), dynasty (朝代), ancient capital (古都), feudal society (封建社会)
  • 动词短语: date/trace back to (追溯到), originate from (来源于), birthplace (发祥地), best-preserved (保存最完好的), be named after (以……命名)
  • 历法: lunar calendar (阴历), solar calendar (阳历), heavenly stem (天干), earthly branch (地支)

06. 经济与管理

  • 宏观: economic globalization (经济全球化), market economy (市场经济), market regulation (市场调节), expand domestic demand (扩大内需), purchasing power (购买力)
  • 企业/贸易: state-owned enterprises (国有企业), private businesses (私营企业), mutual benefit (互惠互利), unfair competition (不公平竞争), customs duty (关税)
  • 金融/就业: interest rate (利率), job hunting (找工作), resume (简历), position (职位)

07. 旅游交通

  • 景点: tourist attraction (景点), places of interests (名胜古迹), natural scenery (自然景观), human landscape (人文景观), royal garden (皇家园林)
  • 体验/交通: broaden one’s horizon (开阔视野), public transport (公共交通), subway station (地铁站), drunk driving (酒驾), passport (护照)

08. 地理环境

  • 位置/地貌: be located in (位于), plateau (高原), peak (山峰), freshwater lake (淡水湖), basin (盆地)
  • 环保/危机: environmental protection (环境保护), sustainable development (可持续发展), carbon emission reduction (碳减排), greenhouse effect (温室效应), endangered species (濒危物种), desertification (荒漠化)
  • 灾害: typhoon (台风), earthquake (地震), flood (洪水), drought (干旱)

09. 教育

  • 体系/学生: nine-year compulsory education (九年义务教育), higher education (高等教育), dropout (失学儿童), enrollment rate (入学率), college entrance examination (高考)
  • 学位/教学: bachelor’s degree (学士学位), compulsory course (必修课), elective course (选修课), credit system (学分制), teaching faculty (师资力量)
  • 改革: exam-oriented education (应试教育), quality education (素质教育)

10. 科技

  • 互联网: search engine (搜索引擎), netizen (网民), information explosion (信息爆炸), be addicted to (上瘾), e-commerce (电子商务), core technology (核心技术)
  • 航天/其他: space laboratory (空间实验室), detector (探测器), innovation (创新), artificial intelligence (人工智能)

💡 最后冲刺建议

  1. 动词优先： 着重记忆“一词多译”的情况（如“实现”可以是realize或achieve），以及“开展/实施”对应的carry out短语动词。
  2. 主题串记： 不要孤立背单词。例如，记住“Temple of Heaven (天坛)”后，连带记忆 pray (祈求)、sacred (神圣的)、architecture (建筑)。
  3. 关注真题规律： 近年CET-6翻译偏重中国文化（节日、工艺）、社会发展（环保、经济） 和科技生活（互联网、AI），请重点复习第三部分中的01、02、03、08、10章节。
  4. 低概率防身： 对于“冷门但可能考”的词（如canyon峡谷, manuscript手稿），只需做到看到中文能想到英文即可，不必强求拼写完美。

祝您2026年6月CET-6考试高分通过！
