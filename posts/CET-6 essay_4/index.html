📝 英语写作实用句型与词汇整理

一、核心首句结构

From my perspective, it is only by [动词-ing + 宾语] that [主语] can [结果].

二、现象论述框架

引出观点

This phenomenon is not accidental but can be attributed to several factors.
To begin with, 论点1.
What’s more, 论点2.
Last but not least, 论点3.

强调重要性

It is universally acknowledged that…
Certainly no one can dispute the evident truth that…

三、主题作用与影响

功能 句型
重要作用 主题词 play a vital role in 某方面
正面/负面影响 主题词 have beneficial / harmful influence on 某方面
双重作用 Not only does 主题词 increase 某人某方面, but also enables them to 某方面

常用动词： increase / bring / facilitate / enhance

四、结论段模板

In conclusion, the importance of paying closer attention to 主题 cannot be overstated.
Instead of ______, it is wiser to ______, not merely for ______, but for ______.

主体 is required to exert all efforts to 做某事 and make 受关联的事物 better.
In this manner, hopefully, we（或主体） may have a promising prospect.

五、论证方法（论点展开三要素）

方法 内容
1. 具体化 是什么？在什么场景？哪些人？怎么做？什么特征？
2. 因果/影响 为什么会有这个影响？会带来什么影响？
3. 对比/反证 如果没有会怎样？

六、高分句型模板

必要性表达

X has become a prerequisite / necessity / essential factor for Y.
什么能力 + 对什么重要，because…

对比建议

Instead of [错误做法] , we ought to [正确做法] .

让步转折

While [正面价值] is undeniable, it is equally important to recognize [潜在问题] .

要求表达

require + [主体] + to [高要求] + rather than merely + [低要求]

结果表达

making it + [形容词] + for sb to do…

风险警示

Without X, … risk being left behind.

七、高频实用词汇

数量程度

中文 英文
很多/越来越多 an increasing number of / considerable
绝非 by no means

能力与转变

中文 英文
使我们能够 empower(s) us to
从……转变为…… have evolved from…into…
具备 with
区分 distinguish between

积极拥抱 actively embrace
保持竞争力 remain competitive
快速变化的世界 a rapidly changing world

比喻表达

save valuable time（节省宝贵时间）

对比连接

by contrast（相反）

八、动词升级替换表

基础词 高分替换

help enable / empower / facilitate / allow
get acquire / obtain / gain
make generate / produce / create / foster
show demonstrate / illustrate / reveal / indicate
have possess / be equipped with / boast
be important be essential / crucial / vital / indispensable
do / can be done can be handled / carried out / performed / accomplished
like / enjoy embrace / appreciate / value
need require / demand / call for / necessitate
want expect / desire / seek / aspire to

九、开篇背景句

In recent years, [现象/问题] has become a growing concern in our society.

With the rapid advancement of technology, [科技事物] has become an integral part of our daily lives.

There is no denying that [品质/能力] plays a vital role in one’s overall development.

Like a double-edged sword, [科技/现象] has both positive and negative impacts on our lives.
